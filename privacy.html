1 Who this applies to

This notice describes how information may be handled when you download, open, or use Glamify: Dress Up Studio (the “App”) on Apple devices. It is meant to complement the privacy disclosures shown on the App Store and any separate policy published by the developer.

2 Categories of information

Depending on how you use the App and which features or integrations are active, the following types of data may be processed (as also summarized on the App Store product page):

  • Identifiers — such as device or advertising-related identifiers where applicable.
  • Usage data — such as interactions with the App, session or feature usage, and similar analytics signals.
  • Diagnostics — such as crash logs, performance data, or technical reports used to keep the App stable.

Some of this data may be associated with your account or device (“linked to you”), while other data may be processed in a way that is not linked to your identity, depending on implementation and platform settings.

3 How we use information

Information may be used to:

  • Operate dress-up, avatar creation, coloring, and related in-App experiences.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud or abuse, and enforce acceptable use.
  • Measure engagement, improve features, and fix bugs or crashes.
  • Deliver or measure advertising or attribution where permitted by platform rules and your settings.

4 Tracking & advertising context

The App Store listing indicates that certain data (for example, identifiers and usage data) may be used to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies, subject to Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework and your device choices.

You can review and adjust tracking preferences in iOS/iPadOS settings (for example, under Privacy & Security). Exact behavior may change with App updates and platform policies.

5 Sharing & service providers

We do not sell your personal information in the ordinary sense of exchanging it for money. We may share data with vendors that help us host, analyze, secure, or distribute the App (for example, analytics or advertising partners), where those parties process data under contractual obligations and applicable law.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of users and services.

6 Families & younger players

Glamify is rated for broad audiences on the App Store. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about your child’s use of the App, you may use the contact options below. We encourage families to use Apple’s parental controls and Screen Time features to manage downloads and purchases.

7 Your choices & regional rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of personal data, or to object to specific uses. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise rights that apply to you, contact us using the information in the next section.

8 Updates to this notice

We may revise this page from time to time. When we do, we will change the effective date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated notice, except where your consent is required for new processing.
